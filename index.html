Welcome to Share A Flight

Where the dream of aviation becomes a reality!

Join our community to find a local plane to rent, or come take a ride with another member. Our goal is to make general aviation affordable and accessible to pilots who don't have the capital to own their own aircraft. We do this by creating a community of airplane owners who want to lend their planes to pilots who are not owners themselves. Planes are expensive and many of them do not get utilized for much of the year, racking up hangar and maintenance fees, when they could be enjoyed by other pilots nearby. This allows owners to recoup some of the expenses of ownership, and opens up the rental options outside of the airport FBO. We also encourage pilots to coordinate shared flights which allows our members to share the costs of a flight while they fly together.
